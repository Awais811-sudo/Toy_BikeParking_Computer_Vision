How It Works

Our AI-powered bike parking system simplifies the parking experience through smart technology and user-friendly processes.

Step 1: Cameras Monitor Available Slots

Our AI-powered cameras continuously monitor the parking area, detecting available slots in real-time using computer vision and YOLO object detection.

Step 2: Users Reserve Online

30% of slots are available for online reservation. Users can book a slot through our mobile app or website before arriving at the parking facility.

Step 3: Secure Entry with Verification

Upon arrival, users verify their identity with CNIC and the system scans their license plate for enhanced security and tracking.

Step 4: Pay via Mobile Wallet or Cash

Choose your preferred payment method - JazzCash, Easypaisa, or traditional cash payment for a seamless experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a parking slot?

You can reserve a parking slot through our mobile app or website. Simply create an account, select your preferred location, choose an available slot, and complete the reservation process.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments through JazzCash, Easypaisa, and cash. Digital payments provide a faster and more convenient experience.

How secure is the parking facility?

Our facilities are equipped with 24/7 surveillance cameras, and license plate recognition for enhanced security. Only authorized users can access the parking area.

What happens if I stay longer than my reserved time?

Additional charges will apply for extended stays beyond your reserved time. The system will automatically calculate the extra time and add it to your payment.

About Us

Our Story

Wheel Wise Vision was founded with a vision to revolutionize bike parking through AI technology. What started as a small project has now grown into a comprehensive parking solution.

Our mission is to promote cycling as a sustainable mode of transportation by providing cyclists with peace of mind knowing their bikes are safe and secure while they're away.

We believe that by removing the worry of bike theft, more people will choose cycling for their daily commute, contributing to reduced traffic congestion and a cleaner environment.
